The way this is animated is quite amazing. It's really a piece of art.

Cinema Paradiso

The overall movie had so much impact that it would inspire an aspiring filmmaker to start filming.

Hugo

Really beautiful story. The way the movie was shot was incredible.

Grave of fireflies

Amazing 2d animated movie by studio ghibli, the story and the work really depicts the outcome of the world war and how it affected the families and people of japan

Kung fu Panda

The animated movie about the panda who has self doubt about himself, slowly learning to master his confidence and giving his all. Best to say super humourous, i was laughing till my stomach hurt. Best laugh i ever had.

Numafung

It's a movie about the cultural depiction of limbu people in Nepal. The movie was released in 2001 but i had the chance to watch it nearly after 17 years if it's release. They have made a re-make of it with the movie name "Jaari" which also has my heart. The movie discuss about the limbu culture and their tradition through the seer method of depiction by using two distinctive characters and the plot story.
